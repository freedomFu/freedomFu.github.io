<p style="font-size:16px;">Hi.🫡 I am currently a third-year Eng.D candidate at Nankai University, under the supervision of [Prof. Ding Wang](http://wangdingg.weebly.com/). Prior to this, I earned a joint Master of Engineering degree with the University and Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), and Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by [Senior Engineer Qiongxiao Wang](https://dblp.org/pid/52/8379.html) with significant guidance from [Associate Professor Bingyu Li](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Bingyu-Li-12). I was also part of the research team led by [Prof. Jingqiang Lin](https://lin-jingqiang.github.io/). My research interests include identity authentication, measurement, and web security.
