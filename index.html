Biograph

I am a Eng.D student at Nankai University under the supervision of Prof. Ding Wang. Before that, I have received the joint Master of Engineering degree with the Uninversity and Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), and Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Senior Engineer Qiongxiao Wang, and I have been in the team under the direction of Prof. Jingqiang Lin. My research interests include identity authentication and password-based authentication. I have been interning with Associate Professor HuaWei Mei's Dreamtech group since my sophomore year in NCEPU.
