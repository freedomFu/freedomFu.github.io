Hello there🫡! Wish you have a nice day 😁

My name is Yanduo Fu (付彦铎 in Chinese). I am currently a third-year Eng.D candidate at Nankai University, fortunate to be supervised by Professor Ding Wang. Prior to this, I earned a joint Master of Engineering degree with the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), and the Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Senior Engineer Qiongxiao Wang in the research team led by Professor Jingqiang Lin. During this period, I also participated in an academic seminar led by Associate Professor Bingyu Li. Additionally, I have been interning with Associate Professor Huawei Mei’s Dreamtech group since my sophomore year at North China Electric Power University (NCEPU) in Baoding.

🎯 My research fields mainly include password-based authentication, password manager, end-to-end testing. Additionally, I’m currently intrigued by the fields of network measurement and digital forensics. I aspire to leverage technical skills to safeguard the security of the internet and the privacy of users.

✨ In life, I prefer to engage in some gentle exercises, such as health-preserving basketball, yoga, strength training, jogging, reading, and listening to the same piece of music repeatedly. I also occasionally play single-player games (e.g., NBA 2K series).
